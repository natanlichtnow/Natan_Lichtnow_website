Transition and relaunch

Over time, during the pandemic and with the Flow arts influence, Natan slowed tempos from the fast beats of psytrance to the slow waves of triphop and downtempo. In 2024 he retired the Mr Kane project and relaunched as Natan Lichtnow, focusing on instrumental live loops on Twitch.
