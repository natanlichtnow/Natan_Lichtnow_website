Flow Arts

Flow arts ~ the art of dance with object manipulation

First contact

Natan started his flow arts journey influenced by the community of artists who came to Twitch during the pandemic, his first contact with this art form felt magical!

With a lack of resources and going through a hard time, Natan made a poi for himself using paper balls, tape and strings, and thats how his journey begun!

Community & growth

Quickly his Twitch community felt his love for the art form and started supporting his journey, getting him his first LED POI set, allowing him to progress even faster, Natan started his small flow community with some friends where they would come together to practice and share tricks digitally, as well as DJing on stream and practicing flow to add sweet visuals to the music

Training with professionals

As natan had a international love story (read the full story to get context) he had incredible opportunities to be trained by professionals, working together with the Luminosity fire team and the Moving Fire Arts team at first as a fire safety but having random opportunities to perform himself as part of the teams)

Boom Festival

At boom festival, Natan had the craziest opportunities to perform together with Grouch in DUB, an experience that completely changed his perspective on this artform

Watch the full set here

Solo shows

With all this beautiful experience in Europe, Natan started doing his own solo shows in Brazil

Retreat & return

With all his goals quickly being met with this artform, Natan felt satisfied with the journey he had, deciding then to retire from the performance world to focus on his Twitch & music career again, but everytime he has a chance, he brings his poi around and shares his love for this artform
