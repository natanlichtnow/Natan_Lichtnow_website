What the stream should feel like

Less broadcast, more room.

The strongest streams feel conversational, exploratory, and present. This page now frames the channel that way instead of treating it like a utility link.

Block 01

Live music sessions

Improvised or structured sets that let people hear the atmosphere in a more immediate and unfinished state.

Block 02

Process and experiments

Trying ideas in public, showing what works, what fails, and what the creative process actually looks like between polished outputs.

Block 03

Audience interaction

A space where community shapes tempo, topics, and energy instead of staying completely outside the work.

How this page works now

The structure now reads like a real live-channel page. It explains the role of the stream, the kind of content people can expect, and how the channel fits the larger music and flow arts universe.
