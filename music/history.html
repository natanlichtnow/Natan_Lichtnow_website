Music Story

A story of highs and lows, full of vulnerability and challenges, that continues to be written day after day.

Chapter 01 01

Introduction

The story begins here.

After The Fall in the paraglider universe, Natan found refuge in psytrance parties, initially following an unknown and even wrong path inside that world.

It did not take long before, on a very specific day, his life changed direction completely.

Open paraglider story
Chapter 02 02

How it all started

One day, at one of those parties, Natan was feeling uncomfortable. Since he could not go home in that state, he decided to go to his friend Birow's house to spend the night.

When he arrived, his friend picked up a small controller and started playing, introducing the world of DJing to the young man.

Natan's eyes lit up. It was love at first sight with that art form.

In that moment it was decided: that would be his life from then on.

Chapter 03 03

A favor that changed everything

Birow was amazed by his friend's passion and decided to lend him the controller for a few weeks.

Natan felt life coming back into him through psytrance frequencies, dedicating countless hours each day to learn as much as possible while he had that controller at home.

Chapter 04 04

An angel

2017

After a few weeks, Birow asked for the controller back. Natan then had a conversation with his grandmother about his experience with DJing.

She confessed she had never seen him that excited about something and decided to invest in his dream, buying his first pair of studio monitors, headphones, and his own controller.

From then on, his life was never the same.

Chapter 05 05

Kill All Negative Energies (Mr Kane)

Late 2017 - 2018

Still carrying pain and confusion related to The Fall, Natan had the idea of creating a character, a mask, to build a new reality.

That character's name: Mr Kane, Kill All Negative Energies.

His focus was melodic progressive trance, with a spiritual and positive approach, seeking to help those who connected with it feel a little better in that magical dancefloor moment.

Chapter 06 06

A fast beginning

It did not take a week after launch for Mr Kane to close his first gigs.

His melodic and smooth sound stood out in a scene full of heavier tracks, calling the attention of both audience and local event contractors.

He was very well received by collectives like Bora Psicodelizar and Trance On, and played his first sets at AOCA Bar.

Chapter 07 07

Constant evolution

It did not take long for Natan to feel the need to produce his own music.

With few resources and limited musical knowledge, the beginning was challenging.

Once again, in a conversation with his grandmother, she decided to invest in him and enrolled him in Yellow Academy in Curitiba.

Chapter 08 08

Music life was not enough

Natan needed financial resources.

Even with fast evolution in the scene, he was financially undervalued at the beginning of his career. That led him back to working with his father at the paragliding school.

His routine was intense: during the week, around 10 hours per day of production and study, plus 3 hours of DJ practice. At night he rested. On weekends, he worked at the school or played events when there were bookings.

Interlude 01 I

An unpleasant surprise

Natan clearly remembers a very disappointing day.

After finding a new light in music during a difficult period, he told his father with pride: Dad, I am going to be a DJ.

His father answered: Drop that. To be a musician you need dedication, and if there is one thing you are not, it is dedicated.

That hurt him deeply.

Chapter 09 09

The evolution that caused conflict

2019

In 2019 his career started growing.

First trips arrived, playing through countryside areas in Parana, Sao Paulo and Santa Catarina, being hired and finally receiving his first meaningful fees.

But this started generating conflict with his father, since lessons at the school and events both happened on weekends.

Then came the ultimatum: either music or paragliding work. The answer was clear: music.

Chapter 10 10

Turnaround

January 2020

In January 2020, Natan went through inguinal hernia surgery. After one month and a half of recovery, he returned to the stage.

His last gig before the break was at Lunna Trance in Curitiba. April promised a full month, with events every weekend, travel and good fees.

Then the pandemic came, and everything stopped.

Chapter 11 11

What now?

Without work, without events and without income, living from emergency aid, his reality changed drastically.

Even so, he stayed firm in studies and music production, preparing for a return that never happened.

Chapter 12 12

The second fall

A few months after the start of the pandemic, one of the greatest losses arrived: the death of his grandmother, the person who supported him most, the only one in the family.

From that point on, the timeline became blurry in his memory. Events were intense and consecutive, hard to process.

Interlude 02 II

Necessary context

After his grandmother passed, his grandfather moved in with his mother. During that period, his mother started a relationship with an extremely traditional man who constantly clashed with Natan.

Living together became increasingly difficult, evolving from arguments to extreme tension, eventually including violent threats.

From that point on, Natan's life became turbulent. Whenever that man was home, he took refuge in his music studio.

Music stopped being only passion. It became survival.

Chapter 13 13

A new opportunity

Everything started changing when Natan saw Tiago Sena, 4i20, streaming on Twitch.

Inspired, he started his own streams, first with low reach, producing music and playing games.

Chapter 14 14

A dream came true, but...

Over time, he built a bond with Tiago, who became his mentor in music production.

His dream of releasing on Alien Records became real.

But something changed: his music taste slowed down drastically. He entered internal conflict, unable to produce the sound he once loved.

Chapter 15 15

There is still hope

During his streams, he met a girl who practices flow arts. They quickly developed a friendship and started collaborating.

Natan on the music, she on the visuals with her hypnotizing dance.

His community got curious to see how his music was made, and asked Natan to stream in English to share the creative process.

With his shy English, Natan gave this new opportunity a chance, and that is where his Twitch career truly began.

Chapter 16 16

Flow Arts: object dance manipulation

late 2021

In the middle of chaos at home, crazy opportunities started appearing. First, he was gifted a notebook by a fan.

That opened a world of online work possibilities: editing videos and working on more complex music projects. Natan started rebuilding himself and saving money to leave that bizarre situation.

At the same time, during his musical confusion, he developed a passion for something that had never crossed his mind before: flow arts.

Chapter 17 17

Movement

During his music crisis, Natan dedicated all his time to learning this new art form. It was incredible, a form of exercise that relieved his mind from all the chaos.

The friendship with that girl also kept growing, sharing online training sessions. In between, he edited videos for other streamers and worked as a freelance music educator, plus mix and master jobs for producers.

He became so involved in that online life that the idea of returning to stages started fading away.

Chapter 18 18

Time for change

Natan slowly saved money to leave that situation with his mother. When he tried to talk to his family about what he was living and how he felt, he was constantly disrespected and dismissed.

He made one last push on his streams to move out, and it finally happened. He was free from that torture at his mother's home.

Now living alone, in an empty house with limited resources but peace in his mind, he started reflecting on the direction of his life. Then something unexpected happened.

Chapter 19 19

Love?

January 2022

That girl had become his best friend. They even called each other sister and brother, very present in each other's process. Over time, that energy began to change.

After an honest conversation, they reached a conclusion: they were in love.

She in the Netherlands, he in Brazil.

This was January 2022. They decided to give themselves until June to meet. If it did not happen, they would drop the idea of that impossible love.

Natan set a stream goal of 1500 euros, but could not publicly explain exactly what it was for.

Chapter 20 20

The impossible is only possible if you believe

April 2022

It was April. Only 150 euros had been saved, and things looked pessimistic.

Then the craziest thing in his life at that point happened: a stranger entered the girl's stream and started hitting all goals on screen. Then he wrote in chat, Who here has a dream?

Natan quickly replied and started a conversation with that stranger. It was unusual. He shared his reality.

That stranger was a Finnish air traffic controller who had suffered a lot in life, made more money than he needed, and had started helping Twitch people make dreams come true.

After hearing Natan's story, he did not hesitate and made an insane donation of 1500 euros while Natan was offline. The dream of meeting his new love became possible.

Chapter 21 21

The first meeting

Natan was confused but very excited. In six months he had left a miserable situation at his mother's house and was about to cross the ocean to meet someone who could be the love of his life.

No one around him believed this was really happening. His family did not believe in his potential. It was a surprise to everyone.

It was his first time on an airplane, 13 hours of travel. That first hug on arrival felt like a fairy tale, an indescribable sensation.

She took him to a coffee shop, they got quality weed, and went home. After six months in a long-distance relationship, the following days were intense.

Chapter 22 22

The incredible kept happening

Summer 2022

Natan and the girl developed a flow arts workshop and got space to present it at TwitchCon in Amsterdam.

Then, under her influence, they drove to Portugal on the way to BOOM Festival. That is a giant story on its own and impossible to fully describe in text.

You can watch the highlight from his livestream, a 40-minute story time video.

Open Twitch highlight

After BOOM, Natan played some shows in the Netherlands and Belgium, then went to Germany for Phoenix Fire Convention, where he received strong training in fire manipulation and became ready to start his own career in Brazil when he returned.

Chapter 23 23

Return to Brazil and the future plan

Natan returned to his empty house, without furniture, after three months of absolute fairy-tale events, carrying a huge baggage and a very positive feeling about that relationship.

At the end of his stay in the Netherlands, they had a deep conversation about future plans.

She was in a delicate financial situation and decided she would not go to Brazil yet. They would stay eight months apart and meet again in 2023 in the Netherlands. Then she would go to Brazil and they would move together.

The plan was to move to Minas Gerais, spend high season in the Netherlands doing shows and making money, then return to Brazil for a nature-centered life using currency differences in their favor.

Back in Brazil, life was monotonous. He had solo gigs, but nothing close to the intensity of what he had lived overseas.

Chapter 24 24

The second meeting

Those eight months apart were turbulent. The girl went through a crisis and the relationship became very cold.

With the relationship now public, it was easier for Natan to hit the plane-ticket stream goal.

One month before traveling, Natan gave up his house. He was not satisfied with where he lived, and planned to scout a possible future place for both of them after the trip.

He stayed with his father before crossing the ocean again. Everything felt strange. The girl was emotionally distant.

Two weeks before the trip she casually said she felt no excitement for this meeting. Natan vented to his father and heard: stay quiet so this trip does not get canceled. What a piece of advice.

Chapter 25 25

What the hell is happening?

Natan crossed the ocean once more to meet his love, carrying a hole in his heart from the lack of attention.

On arrival he was met with a truckload of work. There was almost no intimacy left in that couple. It was extremely confusing.

They took a week off for Anjuna Deep Festival in Albania. That was where everything turned upside down.

On the way there, she confessed she had met a guy at the previous edition and said he was very attractive. She tried to convince Natan to stay at that guy's house after the festival. That was not pleasant at all. They argued heavily, and she confessed wanting an open relationship, then dropped the topic after seeing how hurt he was.

The festival stay was strange. She showed interest in other people while the romantic relationship they had before was no longer present. Emotional confusion was massive.

When they returned to the Netherlands, Natan was almost denied entry. They were interviewed at customs, she in Dutch and he in English, so officials could compare facts and understand why a Brazilian was returning from Albania to the Netherlands.

That made her afraid: What if we move to Brazil and you cannot enter the Netherlands anymore? Our plan will fail. We need a new plan.

Chapter 26 26

A new plan

A new plan was made in a rush. Instead of her moving to Brazil, Natan would move to the Netherlands. After three years there, he would get a European visa and then they could continue the Brazil plan with better weather and stronger financial conditions.

She wanted to take vacation and visit Brazil. Natan needed to learn Dutch as fast as possible for the visa exam.

He dropped everything and found the perfect house for that period, 20 meters from the beach, surrounded by Atlantic forest, perfect for her vacation and for his extreme focus on learning a language.

She had her Brazil trip booked. After that trip, Natan would return with her to the Netherlands to stay for good.

Chapter 27 27

A beautiful birthday gift

Natan had been living in that house for two months, isolated, focused only on studying Dutch and streaming to support himself, with everything aimed at moving to the Netherlands.

But something bizarre was happening: she showed no interest in his language progress, and distance kept growing between them.

Exactly one week before the visa exam, one week after his birthday, she brought the news: I want a non-monogamous relationship, I do not see this working with you, better to end it.

The exact words are not shareable because it was shocking.

That is how a big dream and a big love ended.

Chapter 28 28

Mr Kane is dead

Natan looked at what his artistic career had become: an impossible love story. In his streams and social media, that story had become his life.

Now that it was over, he did not know how to continue. After a confusing period traveling around, he decided to return to his homeland and paraglider roots, getting closer to family.

He also decided it was time to start playing instruments. He invested in a Native American flute and a tongue drum, and began mastering them. Inspired by constant nature contact, he brought that natural texture into his music.

He kept streaming for another year with a feeling of emptiness and disconnection, still playing DJ sets but now adding live instruments on top, a major difference in his streamer career.

At the end of 2024 he made a decision he never imagined: kill Mr Kane.

Chapter 29 29

I am Natan Lichtnow

2025

Mr Kane was created as an escape from the reality he lived. Natan felt the need to face reality brutally and rebuild himself not only as an artist, but as a person.

He looked at his values and what he was doing, and reached a conclusion that shocked everyone around him: leave DJ life aside, focus one hundred percent on becoming a multi-instrumentalist, create improvised live music with a context of peace and spiritual elevation, without labels and without masks.

This brought deep seriousness to his artistic work. Development was fast, community response was strong, and he got support to invest in instruments he always dreamed about: cello, different flutes, and MIDI controllers. His project was rising.

Chapter 30 30

The Temple

Natan started feeling the light returning inside him, with a strong sense of purpose in the music he was creating. He felt he could help people slow down.

With that in mind, he built the full context for his streams: The Temple.

Life is moving too fast, use this time to slow down became his slogan.

That became his great passion and focus. To this day, with a small but loyal Twitch community, Natan keeps writing his story on the internet, creating a safe atmosphere for people to be vulnerable, connect, and share discoveries from their inner journey.
