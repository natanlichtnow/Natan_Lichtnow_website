Project 01

Selector Homepage

A bilingual landing experience that frames one personal site as three different worlds. The challenge was turning identity into navigation without losing clarity.

  • Problem

    Create a first impression strong enough to separate musician, pilot, and developer without making the homepage feel fragmented.

  • Solution

    A character-selection style landing page with bilingual content, remembered language state, and direct routes into each branch.

Project 02

Paraglider Section System

A branch redesign that moves away from glitch aesthetics and toward a brighter, landscape-driven system with clearer progression pages and experience structure.

  • Problem

    The original internal pages felt too visually shared with the homepage and did not reflect the nature-led tone of flying.

  • Solution

    New atmosphere, stronger copy, sales-style experience pages, and a cleaner narrative hierarchy for the whole pilot branch.
